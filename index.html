Brief Biography

I come from Huai'an (淮安), China. I am currently a Ph.D. student in Department of Electrical Computer and Systems Engineering of Case Western Reserve University and advised by Prof. Pan Li. I received MS and BS degree from Carnegie Mellon University and Nanjing University of Posts & Telecommunications in 2016 and 2014, respectively.

Research Interests

My research interests span data privacy and system security, wireless communication, graph theory, and their applications on the Internet of Things (IoT), Cyber-physical Systems (CPS), and smart city, etc.
